About PICT

Pune Institute of Computer Technology (PICT) is a premier private engineering college in Pune, Maharashtra, India. It was established in 1983 by the Society for Computer Technology and Research (SCTR), in response to the growing need for well-qualified professionals in the field of Information Technology.

© 2024 PICT College

Courses Offered

PICT offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses in Computer Engineering, Information Technology, and Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering.

Undergraduate Courses:

  • Bachelor of Engineering (BE) in Computer Engineering
  • Bachelor of Engineering (BE) in Information Technology
  • Bachelor of Engineering (BE) in Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering

Postgraduate Courses:

  • Master of Engineering (ME) in Computer Engineering
  • Master of Engineering (ME) in Information Technology
